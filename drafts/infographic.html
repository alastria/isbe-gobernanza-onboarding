Core Technology Deep Dive

Understanding the technologies that guarantee security, efficiency, and legal compliance.

Why eIDAS is Essential

The eIDAS regulation provides the legal and technical foundation for trust, offloading complex identity verification to regulated Qualified Trust Service Providers (QTSPs).

The `did:elsi` Advantage

ISBE uses `did:elsi`, a decentralized identifier derived from eIDAS certificates, ensuring it outperforms other methods in legal validity and interoperability.

Certificate of Representation: The Preferred Choice

For signing contractual agreements, ISBE requires a Certificate of Representation. This choice is critical for establishing a direct, auditable link between the legal entity and the individual with the authority to bind it, minimizing legal risk.
